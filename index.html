31st August 1888, 2:30am

During the gloomy night, there are prostitute women, who are walking alone in the dark foggy street around the junction of Osborne Street and Whitechapel High Street.

In the pitch dark corner of the street, there is anonymous man rushing out and grab her neck with his left hand.

With the smile of joy in his face, the anonymous man choked a woman to death.
